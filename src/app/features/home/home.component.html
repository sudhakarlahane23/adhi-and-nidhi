About Adhi & Nidhi

Adhi & Nidhi is an artificial jewellery brand inspired by my two daughters, Adhi and Nidhi.

We offer a carefully selected collection of stylish and affordable artificial jewellery that looks luxurious, elegant, and rich. Every design is chosen with care to help you look your best without spending a lot.

You can shop with us online or visit our store to explore our collection in person. We also offer a flat 20% discount on every item, so you always get beautiful jewellery at a great price.
